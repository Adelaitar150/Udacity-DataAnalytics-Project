What is the structure of your dataset?¶

The dataset contains statistics of the English Premier League 2020/21 season.It has all the players that played in the EPL and their various statistics which includes minutes played, matches started, percentage of passes completed, goals scored and assists created.The total number of players are 531 with their corresponding 17 attributes.
